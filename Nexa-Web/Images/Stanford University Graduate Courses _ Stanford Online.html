Join Stanford's academic community and gain expertise so you can advance your career, break into your desired field, or prepare for advanced study.

Graduate courses are taught by Stanford faculty and experienced industry professionals. Imagine what you can achieve with the power of world-class education. Earn a Stanford University transcript and academic credit on a part-time, flexible schedule.

  • Join a Stanford classroom from your laptop
  • Enroll in a part-time, flexible graduate program
  • Spend about 20-25 hours/week per course, including class time
  • Earn a graduate certificate in as little as 1-2 years
